About Us

MEERA - a world of exquisite gemstones.

MEERA is the latest venture launched under the esteemed banner of SG Gems based out of Jaipur, India. A renowned name in the jewelry industry since 1988 with a legacy of excellence and a commitment for over 35 years as a manufacturer, exporter and importer. We are delighted to present our inaugural catalog, from scintillating diamonds, Peridot , Topaz to vibrant rubies, emeralds and semi-precious stones like Amethyst, Moonstone, Quartz, Iolite, etc offered in various palettes of colors and cuts.

We have successfully served clients across Europe, including Denmark, Germany, Sweden & Norway.
